Dad, I love you.

On Father's Day and always.

This "I love you" is for a thousand different reasons:
for every word of advice you gave,
for the times you picked me up when I fell,
for the times you stood by silently for the lessons I needed to learn on my own,
for all the sacrifices you made that I never noticed.

But most of all father, I love you for being you.

Straight from the heart because you mean so much to me,
Thank you.

Wishing you a Very Happy Father's Day! - Auston

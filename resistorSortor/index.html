About

The resistorSortor is an electronic appliance that automatically organizes and catalogs your resistors.


For many electrical enthusiasts, electricians, engineers, and educators, organizing electrical components becomes a constant and costly headache. For example, sorting resistors, a common electrical component contained in almost every electrical circuit, is an annoying endeavor which can waste dozens of man-hours. Because of their commonality, even after a little electrical work, resistors can fall into a jumbled mess, and it can be impossible to find one of a specific resistance. I solved this issue by creating my product, resistorSortor, an electronic appliance that can take these unorganized components, measure their ratings in Ohms, and sort them. Much like a coin sorter my innovative product can arrange and sort resistors, allowing them to be easily stored and reused in small amounts of time. Replacing the need for burdened workers to reorganize and count resistors by hand.

About Me

Terrell Parker

CEO & Founder

Auston Terrell Parker, son of Edward Parker and Valerie Parker, is a native of Cary N.C. Terrell graduated from Panther Creek High School, where he was a member of the varsity baseball team. He is an active in his community, volunteering at a local food bank and the Miracle League of the Triangle serving individuals with special needs. Terrell plans to major in physics and mechanical engineering.
